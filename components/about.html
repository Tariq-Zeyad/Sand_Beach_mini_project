Our Story

Nestled on the shores of Khan Younis, Sand Beach Restaurant brings the finest Mediterranean dining experience to Gaza.

Since our establishment, we've been committed to serving fresh, locally-sourced seafood and traditional Palestinian cuisine with a modern twist. Our location south of Al-Nus intersection offers unparalleled sea views and a peaceful dining atmosphere.
